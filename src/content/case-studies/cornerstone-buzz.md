Cornerstone Church runs the kind of weekly calendar where there is always something on the schedule. New teaching series, special services, kids ministry events, mission interest meetings, mental health initiatives. The design work touches almost every category they push out to the congregation.

## The work

The teaching series work was the heaviest lift. A brief would arrive weeks ahead with the book of the Bible, the date range, and a short paragraph on the angle. After reviewing stock photography references and mocking up three or four directions, the chosen option moved through to a full size set the same week.

The Genesis series was the bigger one. The church was teaching Genesis 12 through 23 in early 2025, then a continuation series on Genesis 25 through 50 starting in June and running through Advent. Two distinct graphic systems were built that still felt like a family. The first settled on a Milky Way image with mountains behind it. The second series went through a couple of rounds because of a stock image sourcing issue that required finding a clean alternative.

Beyond the teaching series, the steady drumbeat was everything else. Ash Wednesday graphics in three sizes. A Vision Banquet promo with a QR code spot. A Night of Worship graphic. A Christmas schedule slide. A Mission/Vision/Values slide set. The Soul Care Initiative graphics (a mental health series). An International Mission Interest Meeting promo. Summer Serve Days for the pre-teen ministry. An "Attempt Something Big" challenge graphic.
