CenterPoint Church sits in Eagle Mountain, Utah, with the tagline "Making Jesus the center point." The work covers a wide range of multi-ministry projects, all flowing through a structured Google Form intake.

## The work

CenterPoint sent a wide range of projects through the form. Sermon series for the adult congregation (I Believe, Wreck The Roof, Bear Fruit). Youth ministry assets for CP YTH, including a Junior High Pool Party promo and a Volunteer Web Page. Class branding for Dream Team and If/Then leadership tracks. And a constant stream of signage: sandwich board signs, front-of-stage 8.5x11 signs, and Starting Point directional signs.

Some projects were Canva-native rather than Illustrator, since the staff did not have Illustrator licensed. One-page templatized designs were developed for materials the team could edit later. Multi-page documents like full leader guides and calendars were handled with reusable one-page templates that could be applied across documents.

The Google Form intake is what keeps the work moving. Clear briefs in, clear designs out across every ministry.
