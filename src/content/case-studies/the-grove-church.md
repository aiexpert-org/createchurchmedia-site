The Grove Church is in Washington state. The brand colors and fonts were specific (Museo Sans Rounded and a brand green at #019447), and Grove kept that brand discipline tight across every piece.

## The work

Grove ran multiple ministry tracks at once. Sermon series art across Three Years that Changed the World, Not It, Never Gonna Give You Up, Suit Yourself, and others. Kids ministry signage, touch cards, A-Frame parking signs for the Back to School Bash, and a major Discipleship Booklet. Youth ministry assets including a YTH Sunday push, Move Up Sunday, and a large youth ministry sign. Outreach events like iHeart Summer Fest, Fight Hunger, and Respite Day. A standalone Breakfast Club piece. Holiday and seasonal cadence around Easter, Thanksgiving, and the new year.

The work pace tightened as the creative leadership transitioned mid-engagement. The incoming creative director stepped in and ran straight from his first project, and there was no drop in quality, no confusion about brand assets, and no missed deadlines. The outgoing media lead handled the transition in the cleanest way possible: briefing his replacement fully, copying him into active projects before handing off, and making sure the vendor relationship transferred without disruption.

One of the standout single pieces is a vertical 33.5x80 inch youth ministry sign for Wednesday Night YTH and Sunday Breakfast Club. The orientation (vertical, not horizontal) was confirmed, the brand green was tweaked to match the physical sign spec, and the final file went to print clean.
