Community Christian Church goes by CCC. Their tagline is "Helping people find their way back to God." The relationship has gone through two phases: a monthly subscription through December 2024, then per-project work from 2025 onward.

## The work

The subscription phase included office signage, bathroom frames, a Corn Maze Night postcard, a full Christmas service invite mailer with a holiday-hatted dog photo, and C3 Kids signs for the children's department, including vector format with cut lines for the printer.

The per-project phase has been steady. A Summer Kickoff package refresh (bathroom sign, postcard front and back, event slide, all dated for June 1). A Costume Sunday postcard date update. A partner-churches graphic update adding a new location to the list. A logo recreation for a partner church app, rebuilt as a clean vector from a low-resolution raster source.

The work that comes through most often is print. Postcards mailed to the community for big Sunday pushes (Christmas, Summer Kickoff, Costume Sunday, Corn Maze Night). The brand fonts are Gill Sans and Adobe Garamond Pro.
